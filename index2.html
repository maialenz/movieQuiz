Game Rules:

  • Choose your favourite theme and click 'PLAY'
  • The trivia consists of 10 questions
  • For each question, you'll have 4 answers, but only one is correct. Choose wisely!
  • The correct answer will turn green, so you can check if you were right or not!
  • If your asnwer is correct, you will be awarded with 10 points
  • Once you have chosen your answer, click on Next to jump to the next
    • question
  • If you want to restart the game, the Restart Quiz button will bring you to the main page where you'll be able to choose the same or a different theme
  • GOOD LUCK!
